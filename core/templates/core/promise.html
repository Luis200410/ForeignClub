Your first 30 days

A 30-day path that turns English into a shared habit

  1. 01

    Join a circle & share your goals

    Meet your community, choose times that work, and receive the starter phrase cards for the week.

  2. 02

    Practise, laugh, and record together

    Rotate through short conversations, capture clips, and celebrate the wins that happen mid-session.

  3. 03

    Use it in real life & report back

    Take one simple action—call a friend, greet a neighbour—and drop a voice note so the circle can cheer with you.
