Our Promise

We keep English
warm, simple, and shared.

FOREIGN is a community project. If conversation ever feels complicated or lonely, we redesign the experience—because language grows fastest when we grow together.

The Journey

01

Unblock.

The first step isn't learning more words; it's removing the fear of using the ones you already know. We start with "Micro-missions"—low-stakes, high-frequency interactions designed to bypass your inner critic. You'll stop translating in your head and start reacting in the moment.

Weeks 1-4

02

Build.

Once the fear is gone, we build structure. You'll join a "Practice Circle" where you'll tackle real-world scenarios—negotiations, debates, storytelling. You'll get instant, actionable feedback from coaches who focus on clarity and impact, not just grammar rules.

Weeks 5-12

03

Flow.

Fluency is when you stop thinking about the language and start thinking about the idea. In the final stage, you lead. You host sessions, mentor newcomers, and take your skills into the "Wild"—real-world networking events and professional challenges.

Weeks 13+
