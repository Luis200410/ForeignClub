The Method

Three simple loops
keep everyone speaking.

01

The Warm-up

Every week starts with a low-stakes "Warm-up" prompt delivered to your phone. It might be a voice note, a photo caption, or a 30-second video. The goal is to get you speaking before you have time to overthink.

02

The Arena

Mid-week, you enter the "Arena"—your live session. This isn't a lecture. It's a guided conversation lab where you practice specific skills: disagreeing politely, telling a joke, explaining a complex idea.

03

The Cool-down

After the session, you get a "Cool-down" reflection. You listen to your own recording, review coach feedback, and identify one thing to improve for next time. This is where the real learning happens.

The System

Live Arena

The heart of FOREIGN is the live interaction. We believe that language is a performance art, not a textbook subject. Our "Arena" sessions are designed to simulate the pressure and spontaneity of real life, but in a safe, supportive environment.

  • ✓ Neighbourhood circles with rotating hosts
  • ✓ Practice prompts designed for pairs
  • ✓ Collective reflections

Digital Layer

Between sessions, our digital platform keeps the momentum going. It's not just an app; it's your training log. It tracks your streaks, stores your recordings, and connects you with your cohort for async practice.

  • ✓ FOREIGN app with lesson cards
  • ✓ AI rehearsal mirrors
  • ✓ Voice analytics

Momentum

Consistency is the only magic bullet. Our "Momentum" system is gamified to keep you showing up. We celebrate streaks, host monthly showcases, and provide on-demand mentorship when you hit a plateau.

  • ✓ Community feed celebrations
  • ✓ On-demand mentors
  • ✓ Monthly showcase nights

The 12-Week Arc

Weeks 1-4

Phase 1: Unblock

+

The focus is on removing friction. You'll stop translating in your head and start reacting.

  • • Week 1: The Identity Shift. Redefining yourself as a speaker.
  • • Week 2: Narrative Basics. Telling simple stories about your day.
  • • Week 3: Opinion & Preference. Expressing likes/dislikes without hesitation.
  • • Week 4: The First Showcase. A 2-minute lightning talk.
Weeks 5-8

Phase 2: Build

+

We introduce structure and complexity. You'll learn to argue, persuade, and explain.

  • • Week 5: Debate Club. Structuring an argument.
  • • Week 6: The Art of the Question. Leading conversations.
  • • Week 7: Nuance & Tone. Softening the blow and polite disagreement.
  • • Week 8: Mid-term Project. A 5-minute presentation.
Weeks 9-12

Phase 3: Flow

+

Total immersion. You take the lead and handle high-pressure scenarios.

  • • Week 9: Improvisation. Thinking on your feet.
  • • Week 10: Negotiation. Getting what you want.
  • • Week 11: Storytelling Mastery. The hero's journey.
  • • Week 12: Graduation. The Final Keynote.
